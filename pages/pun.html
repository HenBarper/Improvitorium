Pun Games

These games involve coming up with jokes and puns from the backline one at a time based of a simple suggestion.

  • 185

185

Description

  • Players get a suggestion and one at a time step forward and deliver a joke or pun with the format below.

Example

Suggestion: Banana

  • 185 bananas walk into a bar.
  • The bartender says, we don't serve bananas here.
  • The 185 bananas say, what? are we too unappealing for you?

Alternate Version:

  • 185 bananas walk into a bar.
  • The bartender says, we don't serve bananas here.
  • The 185 bananas say, why not?
  • The bartender says, you're too unappealing.
